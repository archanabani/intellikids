OUR STORY

Born from a simple belief

Intellikids Academy was founded with one powerful idea: every child deserves a teacher who truly gets them.

We saw a gap between big tutoring platforms — where kids became just a roll number — and what families actually needed: a teacher who knows their child's name, their pace, their struggles, and their dreams.

So we built Intellikids Academy — where Grade KG–12 students get live, personalised sessions in Coding, Maths, English, Physics, Chemistry and more, taught by certified experts who genuinely love teaching.

🎯

Our Mission

To make world-class, personalised education accessible to every curious kid — regardless of geography, background, or learning style.

🌟

Our Vision

A world where every child finds their intellectual superpower and becomes a confident, curious, compassionate thinker and creator.

💡

Our Promise

No templates. No one-size-fits-all. Every class is built around your child — their goals, their gaps, their pace.
